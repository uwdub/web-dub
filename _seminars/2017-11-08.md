To explore this, my research group contributes novel interactive systems to better understand 1) how to productively select and build on the most promising and creative ideas; 2) how to effectively engage in large-scale participatory design by gathering feedback from communities of stakeholders; and 3) how to engage citizens in decision-making processes related to civic issues.
